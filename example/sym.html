Encrypt or Decrypt a message

To encrypt a text, enter it in the text input, enter the password to use for encryption and press "Encrypt". The ciphertext will appear in the output below the buttons.
To decrypt a text, enter it in the text input as well, and, if you enter the correct password before pressing "Decrypt", you'll see the (decrypted) cleartext in the text output.

This page encrypts using AES, with a keysize of 256 bit, in OCB2 mode, with an authentication strength of 128 bit, and 1000 PBKDF2 iterations improving your password.
However, even if this encryption is pretty strong - security depends on your password. No encryption, no matter how strong, will make up for a weak or easy to guess password.
